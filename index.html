Who I Am

My Story

I'm Zanden, a high school student with a relentless passion for science, technology, and making a difference. My journey kicked off at a young age when I started tinkering with computers and coding—my first project was a basic program to explore my interests in STEM. That curiosity has since propelled me into the world of artificial intelligence and biomedical innovation.

At 13, I won the Discovery Education 3M Young Scientist Challenge in 2018 with an AI tool that transformed pancreatic cancer treatment. Now, I’m a neuroscience enthusiast aiming to blend medicine and technology to solve some of the world’s toughest challenges.

What Drives Me

I’m fueled by a desire to improve lives through innovation. My dream is to cure pancreatic cancer, and I’m working toward that by combining AI with healthcare solutions. I also love inspiring others, especially young students, to dive into STEM and think big.
